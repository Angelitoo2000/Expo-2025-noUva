Popular Majors

Software Developer

Develops, tests, and maintains software applications for web, mobile, or desktop platforms.

  • Programming (Java, Python, JavaScript, etc.)
  • Problem-solving and logical thinking
  • Team collaboration and communication

Data Scientist

Analyzes large sets of data to extract insights and support decision-making using statistical methods and machine learning.

  • Data analysis and statistics
  • Programming for data (Python, R, SQL)
  • Data visualization and communication

Marketing Specialist

Plans, executes, and monitors marketing campaigns to promote products or services effectively.

  • Strategic planning and campaign management
  • Market research and analysis
  • Communication and creativity
